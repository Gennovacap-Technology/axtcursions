Float the river!

Tubing is closed for the 2014 season and restarts in May 2015. We are more than happy to take group reservations for 2015.

Ride a tube down the river with ATXcursions. On every trip:

  • Priority access - Skip the line, pick up your tube, get straight in the river! It's the most convenient way to float.
  • Tube rental included - All of our prices include tube rental for you and your guests as well as the cost of cooler tube rental. Coolers float with you on their own tubes =)
  • 3-5 hour float - a perfect amount of time to have fun on the river.
  • Relax while we drive - We're your designated driver! We pick you up from your hotel/airbnb/house/etc, drive you to the river, pick you up at the end, and bring you back. Serving Austin and surrounding areas.
  • We pack your alcohol/snacks/water in ice-filled coolers that we provide for you.
  • The Salt Lick BBQ - an optional (and recommended) stop we can take you to at the end of your trip. Award-winning BBQ for a truly Texas experience.
