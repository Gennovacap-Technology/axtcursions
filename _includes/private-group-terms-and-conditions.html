ATXcursions Terms and Conditions

Payment Policy

50% non-refundable deposit is due at the time of booking. Reservations are not guaranteed until the deposit is made. The remaining 50% balance is due 7-days prior to the date of the scheduled trip, and will be automatically charged to the card on file unless otherwise specified.

Cancellation Policy

We do not issue refunds for cancellations once a reservation is made. We do not issue refunds for no-shows. If you do not show up for your trip, no refund will be issued. No exceptions.

Refund Policy

We do not issue refunds.
