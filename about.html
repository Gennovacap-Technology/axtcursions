Questions and Answers

Where do you pick up?

We pick you up and drop you off directly at your hostel.

What should I bring?

Check out the page for each trip to get details about items you should bring with you. We recommend bringing comfy shoes and a camera on every trip.

How do I pay?

When we arrive to pick you up, you can pay by cash or credit card.

Do I have to pay in advance?

No! We currently do not accept advanced payment.

Should I book in advance?

Absolutely! We do not show up to hostels unless there is a booking.

How much do I need to tip?

Nothing! There is no tipping allowed in The Hostel Shuttle.

Do you need my phone number?

No, but if there is a trip cancellation we will only be able to notify you of the cancellation via email.

Can you take wheelchairs?

At this time we are not wheelchair accessible.

What if I only want to go one-way?

Please let your driver know and if you booked online, note it in the "Special Requirements" section of the booking form. We don't offer discounts for one-direction modifications to trips at this time.

How can I book?

Online or by phone
